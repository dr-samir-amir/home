Short Bio

(A Longer Bio and a Résumé are available separately.)

Samir Amir holds a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Lille (2011). Prior to that, he received Engineering degree (2005) and Master's degree from the University of Rennes (2006). His research activities started in 2008 when he joined the CNRS (French National Research Center) as researcher where he spent more than three years working as part of several European projects (ITEA-CAM4Home, ITEA-MIDAS, …). In 2011, he held an assistant professor position at the University of Lille. Samir was a postdoctoral researcher at Database Research Group of the University of Lyon from 2013 to 2015. Currently, he is Data Scientist and R&D leader at ContentSide where his current research interests cover machine learning and natural language processing.
